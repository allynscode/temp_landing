About

Temp was created to make habit and mood tracking effortless. We believe in keeping things simple, private, and focused—so you can actually understand your routines and build balance without distraction.

FAQ

Is temp free?
Temp is free to use. We're working on a paid tier with more features.
What platforms are supported?
IOS for now. Android and web are on the roadmap.
Do I need an account to use Temp?
No—Temp uses anonymous authentication, so you can start tracking right away.
Does Temp use AI?
Yes—Temp uses AI to generate personalized insights from your daily log, helping you spot patterns in your mood and routines.
